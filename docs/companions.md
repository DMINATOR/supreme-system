# Companions

Detailed mechanics for [companions](glossary.md#companion) — the NPC members of the player's [party](glossary.md#party).

## Acquiring Companions

- Recruited by finding them during world exploration — TBD (events, locations, quests?)
- Recruitment conditions — TBD

## Dismissal

- [Companions](glossary.md#companion) can be dismissed by the player at any time
- The [party](glossary.md#party) roster can change freely over the course of a [run](glossary.md#run)

## Decks & Cards

- Each [companion](glossary.md#companion) has their own individual [deck](glossary.md#deck)
- Each [companion](glossary.md#companion) has a unique [companion deck limit](glossary.md#companion-deck-limit) — the maximum number of cards their deck can hold, which varies per companion
- Some cards are unique to specific [companions](glossary.md#companion)
- [Companions](glossary.md#companion) can acquire new unique cards by progressing through their own [companion quest line](glossary.md#companion-quest-line)

## Combat

- Each [companion](glossary.md#companion) takes their own turn sequentially in combat, playing from their own [deck](glossary.md#deck)
- Each [companion](glossary.md#companion) has their own individual HP pool
- A [companion](glossary.md#companion) can die mid-combat
- If all [party](glossary.md#party) members die, this results in a [party wipe](glossary.md#party-wipe) and ends the [run](glossary.md#run)
