# Hand — Open Questions

> See [hand_spec.md](hand_spec.md) for current settled decisions.

- **Hand size** — how many cards does a party member draw into their hand each turn?
- **Draw behavior** — is a fresh hand drawn at the start of each turn, or does the hand persist between turns with cards replenished?
- **End of turn discard** — what happens to unplayed cards at the end of a turn — discarded, returned to deck, or held?
