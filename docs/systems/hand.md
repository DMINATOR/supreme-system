# Hand

> Facts, rules, behaviors, and relationships: [hand_spec.md](hand_spec.md)

The hand is the subset of cards drawn from a party member's [deck](deck.md) that are available to play on a given turn. Cards in hand are what the player actively chooses from during their turn in combat.
