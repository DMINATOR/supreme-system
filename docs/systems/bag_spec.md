# Bag — Spec

> Structured reference extracted from [bag.md](bag.md). Open questions: [bag_tbd.md](bag_tbd.md).

## Facts

- Bag capacity: 100 cards
- One bag per party — shared across all members

## Rules

- The bag can only be accessed outside of combat (at rest)
- All loot cards go into the bag on acquisition
- Any party member can take cards from the bag or return cards to it
- Cards in the bag cannot be played directly — they must be in a member's deck first

## Behaviors

- Receive card from loot drop
- Transfer card to a party member's deck (at rest)
- Receive card returned from a party member's deck (at rest)

## Relationships

- One bag belongs to the whole party
- Bag is the source pool for all individual decks
