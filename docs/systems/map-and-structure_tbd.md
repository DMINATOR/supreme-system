# Map & Structure — Open Questions

> See [map-and-structure_spec.md](map-and-structure_spec.md) for current settled decisions.

- **Region properties** — What properties does a region carry? Area level range is one; what else? These properties drive enemy density, dungeon/city frequency, and other region-specific behaviour.
- **Master details** — How many masters appear in a city? Is there always exactly one, or can there be multiple offering different benefits? Are masters randomised per city, per run, or fixed?
- **Level-up benefit** — What kind of benefit does a master grant on leveling up? Passive stat boost, a new card, a skill, or something else?
- **Level-up cost** — Is there a cost or requirement to level up at a master (e.g. currency, completing a quest, or is it free)?
- **Final goal specifics** — What is the final dungeon or boss? Where on the map is it located? Does it require unlocking or can it be found naturally by exploring?
