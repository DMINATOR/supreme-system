# Progression & Meta — Open Questions

> See [progression-and-meta_spec.md](progression-and-meta_spec.md) for current settled decisions.

- **Meta progression contents** — What exactly carries over between runs? Candidate options include unlocking new card templates, starting resources, companion roster, or cosmetics. Needs design decision before implementation.
- **Stat scaling formula** — By how much do card stats increase per level? Is it linear, exponential, or per-template? Needs definition before the card system can be implemented.
- **Area level assignment** — How is area level determined for a given zone? Is it fixed per zone, scales with distance traveled, or set by some other metric? Depends on the map and zone design decisions (see [core-loop_tbd.md](core-loop_tbd.md)).
- **Unique card levels** — Unique cards acquired through companion quest lines have no drop zone. What level are they assigned? Options: fixed level, area level at time of award, or companion-specific scaling.
