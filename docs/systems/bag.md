# Bag

> Facts, rules, behaviors, and relationships: [bag_spec.md](bag_spec.md)

The bag is the shared card storage for the entire [party](../glossary.md#party). All [loot](../glossary.md#loot) cards land here first. Party members can freely move cards between the bag and their individual [decks](deck.md), but only during a [rest](../glossary.md#rest-site) — never during combat.

The bag is the primary place to store, review, and redistribute cards between party members between fights.
