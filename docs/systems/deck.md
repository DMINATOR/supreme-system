# Deck

> Facts, rules, behaviors, and relationships: [deck_spec.md](deck_spec.md)

A deck is the personal card collection assigned to a single [party](../glossary.md#party) member. It is the source of cards available to that member during combat. Decks are assembled from cards held in the shared [bag](bag.md) and can only be changed outside of combat.

## Acquiring Cards

Cards enter the deck by being transferred from the shared [bag](bag.md) during a [rest](../glossary.md#rest-site). Additional ways to acquire cards (shops, events, quests) — TBD.
