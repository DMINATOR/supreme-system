# Deck

Card management is split between a single shared [bag](../glossary.md#bag) for the whole [party](../glossary.md#party) and an individual [deck](../glossary.md#deck) for each party member.

## Shared Bag

The [bag](../glossary.md#bag) is a party-wide card pool accessible by all members.

- All acquired cards go into the bag first
- Any [party](../glossary.md#party) member can draw from or return cards to the bag outside of combat
- The bag is the primary way to trade and redistribute cards between party members
- Bag capacity — TBD

## Individual Deck

Each [party](../glossary.md#party) member has their own [deck](../glossary.md#deck) assembled from cards taken out of the [bag](../glossary.md#bag).

- Cards in a member's deck are used to fill [equipment slots](../glossary.md#equipment-slot) and are drawn during combat
- A deck belongs exclusively to that party member for the duration of a combat

### Deck Limit

- Every party member has a [deck limit](../glossary.md#deck-limit) — the maximum number of cards their deck can hold
- The player's deck limit is fixed — TBD value
- Each [companion's](../glossary.md#companion) deck limit varies — see [Companions](../characters/companions.md#decks--cards)

### Composition Rules

- No minimum deck size
- Duplicate cards are allowed — the same card may appear multiple times in a deck

## Acquiring Cards

- Cards are acquired as [loot](../glossary.md#loot) rewards after [encounters](../glossary.md#encounter) and go into the shared [bag](../glossary.md#bag)
- Additional acquisition methods (shops, events, quests) — TBD
