# Deck

> Facts, rules, behaviors, and relationships: [deck_spec.md](deck_spec.md)

Card management is split between a single shared [bag](../glossary.md#bag) for the whole [party](../glossary.md#party) and an individual [deck](../glossary.md#deck) for each party member.

## Shared Bag

The [bag](../glossary.md#bag) is a party-wide card pool accessible by all members. It is the primary way to store and trade cards between party members.

## Individual Deck

Each [party](../glossary.md#party) member assembles their own [deck](../glossary.md#deck) from cards taken out of the [bag](../glossary.md#bag). Cards in a deck fill that member's [equipment slots](../glossary.md#equipment-slot) before combat and are drawn from during combat.

## Acquiring Cards

Cards are acquired as [loot](../glossary.md#loot) rewards after [encounters](../glossary.md#encounter) and go into the shared [bag](../glossary.md#bag). Additional acquisition methods (shops, events, quests) — TBD.
