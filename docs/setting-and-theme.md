# Setting & Theme

## The World

The game takes place roughly 100 years after **[The Ruin](glossary.md#the-ruin)** — a catastrophic collapse of civilization. Governments fell, infrastructure crumbled, and the vast majority of the global population perished. What remains is a fractured world of ruins, wastelands, and struggling survivors who have spent generations adapting to the new reality.

The exact cause of The Ruin is — TBD (options: global nuclear exchange, engineered plague, cascading environmental collapse, or a combination).

## Tone

Gritty survival. The world is harsh and unforgiving, but human connection — finding [companions](glossary.md#companion) and forging bonds — is what keeps people going. Dark, but not without hope. The player's journey is ultimately about perseverance in a world that has already ended.

## Factions

Several [factions](glossary.md#faction) compete for control of resources and territory across the [wasteland](glossary.md#wasteland). See [Factions](factions.md) for the full breakdown.
