# Party

The player travels the world with a [party](../glossary.md#party) of up to 3 [companions](../glossary.md#companion). The party fights together in [encounters](../glossary.md#encounter) and the [run](../glossary.md#run) ends only when all members are dead ([party wipe](../glossary.md#party-wipe)).

## Composition

- The player is always the core member of the [party](../glossary.md#party)
- Up to 2 [companions](../glossary.md#companion) can travel alongside the player (3 total including the player)
- [Companions](../glossary.md#companion) are recruited from the world during a [run](../glossary.md#run)
- [Companions](../glossary.md#companion) can be dismissed at any time
- The [party](../glossary.md#party) roster can change over the course of a [run](../glossary.md#run)

## On the Map

- All [companions](../glossary.md#companion) follow the player visually on the world map
- The [party](../glossary.md#party) moves and explores together as a unit

## In Combat

- Each [party](../glossary.md#party) member has their own individual [deck](../glossary.md#deck) and HP pool
- Combat is turn-based — each [party](../glossary.md#party) member takes their turn sequentially
- A [companion](../glossary.md#companion) can die mid-combat
- The [run](../glossary.md#run) ends when all [party](../glossary.md#party) members are dead ([party wipe](../glossary.md#party-wipe))
