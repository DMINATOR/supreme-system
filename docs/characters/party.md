# Party

> Facts, rules, behaviors, and relationships: [party_spec.md](party_spec.md)

The player travels the world with a [party](../glossary.md#party) of up to 3 [companions](../glossary.md#companion). The party fights together in [encounters](../glossary.md#encounter) and the [run](../glossary.md#run) ends only when all members are dead ([party wipe](../glossary.md#party-wipe)).

## On the Map

- All [companions](../glossary.md#companion) follow the player visually on the world map
- The [party](../glossary.md#party) moves and explores together as a unit
