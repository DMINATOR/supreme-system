# Player Character — Open Questions

> See [player-character_spec.md](player-character_spec.md) for current settled decisions.

- **Deck limit** — what is the player's fixed maximum deck size?
- **Mentor identity** — who was the mentor, what is their history, and what did they know?
- **Destination** — what is the place marked in the journal, and what awaits there?
- **Inherited weapon** — what type of weapon is it, and what is its history?
- **Journal mechanic** — how does the journal work in-game? Is it a UI element, a lore log, does it unlock actively or passively?
