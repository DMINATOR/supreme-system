# Companions — Spec

> Structured reference extracted from [companions.md](companions.md). Open questions: [companions_tbd.md](companions_tbd.md).

## Facts

- Maximum companions in a party: 2
- Each companion has their own deck limit (varies per companion — [TBD](companions_tbd.md) values)
- Each companion has their own HP pool
- Recruitment method: [TBD](companions_tbd.md) (events, locations, quests)

## Rules

- Companions can be dismissed at any time
- A companion can die mid-combat
- Each companion has a set of cards exclusive to them — cannot appear in other decks
- Unique companion cards are acquired by progressing their quest line
- Each companion takes their own turn sequentially in combat

## Behaviors

- Recruit companion from the world
- Dismiss companion from the party
- Take combat turn
- Progress companion quest line
- Acquire unique card from quest line reward

## Relationships

- Companion belongs to one party
- Companion owns one deck
- Companion has one quest line
- Companion has a set of exclusive cards tied to their quest line
